# Gold Layer (Star Schema & Data Marts)

The Gold layer transforms the document-oriented Silver collections into a relational **Star Schema**, optimized for analytical queries, aggregations, and BI tool integration.

## Star Schema Design

The Gold layer is constructed entirely within the `tlc_gold` database in MongoDB.

### Dimensions (Lookup Tables)
Generated by `06_gold_dimensions.ipynb`, these static collections act as the analytical axes:
- **`dim_date`**: Calendar dimension from 2024 to 2025 (resolving `date_id` to day of week, month, etc.).
- **`dim_zone`**: Spatial dimension for the 265 TLC taxi zones.
- **`dim_vehicle`**: Vehicle category lookup (Yellow, Green, FHV, HVFHV).
- **`dim_vendor`**, **`dim_rate_code`**, **`dim_payment_type`**: Categorical code mappings.

### Fact Table (`fact_trips`)
Generated by `07_gold_fact_trips.ipynb`, this massive table is the heart of the Gold layer.
- **Union**: PySpark reads the 4 separated collections from `tlc_silver` and concatenates them into a single DataFrame using `unionByName`.
- **Flattening**: The heavily nested structs from Silver (`datetimes`, `locations`, `financials`) are flattened into top-level columns.
- **Foreign Keys**: String/Categorical properties are mapped to their respective IDs to establish relationships with the dimension tables (e.g., `payment_type_id`, `pickup_date_id`).
- **Default Keys**: For vehicles like FHV that lack certain codes (like a Vendor ID), a fallback key (e.g., `-1`) is assigned to maintain referential integrity with the dimension tables (representing "Unknown" or "N/A").

## Business KPIs (Data Marts)

The `08_gold_metrics.ipynb` notebook acts as an analytical consumer of the Star Schema. It queries `fact_trips` exclusively, joining with dimensions to produce highly aggregated insights:
- **KPI 1: Volume & Revenue**: Aggregating `total_amount` and `trip_distance` grouped by `dim_vehicle`.
- **KPI 2: Congestion Impact**: Summing `cbd_congestion_fee` to analyze the financial impact of the 2025 NYC congestion pricing implementation.
